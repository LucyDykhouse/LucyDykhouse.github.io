About Me

Background

Being from Michigan, my interests include backpacking, biking, and rock hunting around the Great Lakes. I also enjoy picking blueberries and cherries, photography, and gardening. Beyond these interests, I'm a lifelong (aka long-suffering) Detroit Lions fan, and I watch all their games despite the pain they inflict on me. On the left is a picture of me at a Lions' game during their most recent winning season in 2017.

Technical Experience

Prior to pursuing code full time, I worked to model the expanse and availability of water resources across the United States. Some of my favorite projects have been determining a transit time function for water particles in the artificial hillslopes of Biosphere 2 and determining regions' susceptibility to sinkhole collapse by monitoring local streams. Having these experiences with large, complicated data sets has motivated me to consider and work towards a career in data science!

My Projects

BrightPaths Capstone Project

To mark the completion of the first half of the BrightPaths Program, I made an interactive website that functions similarly to how a bullet journal does. The user can add events and occasions, cross off events, and delete events from their monthly and weekly calendars.
